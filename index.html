Unsolicited Advice Tarot

Breathe and relax

Begin to relax and still your mind. Put aside your worries and concerns for now. Settle into the present moment. Take a few deep breaths and relax all your muscles. Take as much time as you need for this calming process.

Think of a question

Open, neutral, and positive questions work best. Ask questions such as: Can you give me insight into... What do I need to understand about... What is the meaning of... What is the lesson or purpose of...

Accept responsibility

Don't ask what to do, tarot can't make the decision for you. Avoid yes or no questions or asking only about time.

Be clear

Find the balance between being too vague and too detailed. Include only the details necessary to make clear what you want to know.

Focus on yourself

Ask about you. If you're concerned about someone else, focus your question on your own role or experience with them.
